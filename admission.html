ADMISSION PROCEDURE

For classes Nursery and LKG are admitted after the interview of candidates and thier parents. For class UKG onwards on the basis of their performance in the admission test.


Subject for Admission Test

For class UKG : Mathematics and English
For class I onwards : English,Mathematics and Hindi

Pupils seeking admission in Class I must complete the age of five(5) years on or before 30th September of the session and should not be more than seven(7) years in age. A corresponding age limit may be calculated for the other classes.
