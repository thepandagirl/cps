GENERAL INFORMATION

To build the carrer of students of the state of Assam at par with the studentsof the other part of the country for All India Competitive Examinations, to provide educational facilities to the students of the state comparable to those provided by the advanced state of India, an educational society in the name of 'Capital Educational Trust' was formed.

The foremost objective of the society is to establish schools under CBSE curriculum with well equipped infrastructure and competent faculty and manpower in various places in the state of Assam. CAPITAL PUBLIC SCHOOL is the one of the schools started by the society. The school was established on 2004 AD and is conveniently located at Pandav Nagar(Boripara), Maligaon, Guwahati-781012

The medium of instruction of school is English and in the school premises only english is to be spoken.
